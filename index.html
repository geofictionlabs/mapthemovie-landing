How it works

Four steps to your reward

No special equipment needed. Just your phone, your movie knowledge, and a sense of adventure.

01
Find a hunt near you
Open the app and browse mystery hunts available near you. Each one hides a destination you discover only on arrival.
02
Solve the movie clues
Answer movie trivia questions where each correct answer fills a digit of the hidden GPS coordinate. Wrong answers cost Signal Points.
03
Follow the GPS compass
Once all clues are solved your compass activates and guides you to the secret location. It glows green when you face the right direction.
04
Claim your reward
Arrive within range and your reward unlocks. Vouchers, prizes, experiences — revealed only when you physically arrive. Staff verify with a PIN.
